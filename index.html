Controls

Use your arrow keys or [W,A,S,D] keys to navigate pacman.

To pause / resume the game press [SPACE] or [ESC] or just click into the game area.

Controls

Use the Arrow Buttons underneath the game area to navigate.

To pause / resume the game, touch the game area once.

Game

Accumulate as much bitcoin as possible and avoid dollar bills to stay alive.

Lightning turns the dollar to shit.
